Dr. Dennis Thom is one of the co-founders of ScatterBlogs, a company specializing in real-time and location-based social media analytics. He is an experienced public speaker presenting since almost a decade at major scientific conferences, entrepreneurship venues, private industry events, and academic lectures. Dennis Thom has authored and co-authored more than 30 papers and book chapters in major peer-reviewed publications. His research interests cover the areas of visual analytics, data mining, information retrieval, and social media monitoring.
